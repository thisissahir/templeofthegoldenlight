we are all surrounded and held by love, joy and care.

temple of the golden light is a free, faith-based church in candler, north carolina. we gather every thursday around sacred cacao and sacred sound to experience god within, through chanting, meditation and community. we are inclusive and non-denominational. everyone is welcome, from any faith or none.

who we are

what we believe

  • in a god of love and care who inspires and brings life to everyone and everything.
  • that god is all names, spirit, the divine, life force energy, consciousness, creator.
  • in a god that moves through every one of us. there is no separation.
  • in a church that is inclusive, gathering around peace, love and care for everyone.
  • in the healing power of sound and gathering, of showing up authentically.
  • that we are loved, held, supported, and have everything we need. always.
